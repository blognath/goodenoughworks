Power BI · DAX · Government Data

India MSME Sector Health Dashboard

The diagnostic question

Which states generate the most employment per MSME enterprise? Where does enterprise density not correlate with credit access? Built a four-page Power BI diagnostic integrating datasets from data.gov.in, DCMSME Annual Reports, and RBI state-wise credit statistics into a unified data model with five custom DAX measures — employment intensity, YoY growth rate, credit-gap index, state performance rank, and share of national total. Produced three actionable consulting findings on enterprise concentration, employment productivity gaps, and financing access disparities across 36 states.

Regulated Procurement Process Diagnostic

The diagnostic question

How does a nuclear-sector procurement process sustain 100% regulatory compliance across multi-vendor, multi-stage workflows? Documented and mapped the end-to-end vendor evaluation and shortlisting process across NPCIL procurement operations — identifying bottlenecks, redundant approval stages, and audit-trail gaps. Redesigned the framework resulting in a 25% reduction in cycle time and 7 consecutive clean AERB audit outcomes. Deliverable: process flow documentation, a compliance checklist matrix, and an Excel-based vendor-tracking tool.
